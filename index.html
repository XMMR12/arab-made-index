About Us

Arab-made believes in the Arab factories’ capabilities and their premium quality. So, we provide a marketplace that connects Arab factories in different industrial verticals to international markets across the world as a sourcing hub to showcase Arab factories’ industrial capabilities, premium products, interests, receive RFQs from buyers and experience the use of multiple different exporting services. Additionally, Arab-made also help streamline and simplify the process of sourcing products for buyers, making it easier and more efficient for them to find the products they need. Arab-Made provides both the international importer and investors with a variety of importing services that help, support, and facilitate their journey in importing from the Arab factories.
